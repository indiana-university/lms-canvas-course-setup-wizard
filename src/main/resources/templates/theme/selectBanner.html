Select banner

Your course home page may include a banner image. If you do not want a banner image on your course home page, select No, then choose Next. If you wish to have a banner image, select Yes, then choose a category to view images. Select the image you want to use and then choose Next.

Note: You may replace the banner image with something from Adobe Stock or another source by editing the course home page. Review the Next Steps document in this course, contact your Campus Teaching and Learning Center, or the ID Clinic for more details.
