Include navigation

You have the option to include navigational elements with your course. The navigation appears beneath the banner image in the example below. It has four quick links that say "Start Here", "Syllabus", "Modules", and "Resources". The link names may be changed. Review the Wizard Next Steps page for detailed instructions or contact your campus teaching and learning center for assistance.

How many buttons on the navigation would you like for the syllabus?
